Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese sandwiches are a great way to make a quick meal with little to no fuss, both in prepping and in cooking! All they require is a pan, some butter, bread, cheese, and a few minutes of spare time, ensuring that you will be able to get all the calories you need with time left to do whatever you need to do the rest of the day. Forget about any of the fuss of meal prep, and get down to doing what you need to do with a quick perk me up!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White bread
  • Scliced Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
